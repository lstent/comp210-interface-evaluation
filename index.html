Standby is a horror game in which the player has to kill the Robot butler whos job it is to clean and care for your home, the robot has realised to destroy the reason the house becomes unclean which is the player, the butler searches, listens and chase the player down eventually killing them. To kill the robot all the keycards scattered around the house have to be found which then allows the player to enter the basement which holds the server, if the player destroys the server they kill the robot. I plan to look in detail at how easily the player can traverse the interfaces available to find the aim screen and use it to help them find the keycards and defeat the game before being found by the killer robot
